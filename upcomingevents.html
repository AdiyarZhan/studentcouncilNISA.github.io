13th of April
Kozy Korpesh and Bayan Sulu's day is a national day of pure romantic love. It is celebrated in honor of the great love between the main characters of Kazakh poem, called "Kozy Korpesh and Bayan Sulu". This day surely helps to promote national customs and values.

30th of March
What? Where? When? is a popular intellectual game, which is annually carried out by the student council of NISA and inspired by a TV show with the same name. It aims to rally our community and make school days more interesting. Moreover, the game contributes to the development of logical thinking and intellectual abilities of our students.
